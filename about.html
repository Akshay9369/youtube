THE ENGINEERING WING



Engineering wing of Sagar Institute of Technology & Management [SITM] makes it an institute with a difference. It has highly qualified and experienced faculty, superb academic infrastructure and academic ambience. Institute provides extensive research oriented activities, workshops, seminars industry onented projects etc.

The Institute has taken cognizance of current technological environment and industry demand. Accordingly, a host of co-curricular activities in technical field and personality development like seminars, workshops, shon programs research work, innovation, paper presentation etc are planned.

The academic activities beyond the syllabi in the recent past include workshops in robotics, JAVA, Trends in Electrical Engineering, Nanotechnology, national seminar on Advances in Science Regional Seminar on Advances in Science and organizing Regional Meet of Children Science Congress etc. A large number of papers have been presented in national and international seminars by the faculty and students.

To encourage technical studies and writing among the students and technical fraternity, the institute publishes three journals: one for Engineering and Applied Sciences, one for Management and one for Pharmaceutical & Herbal Research.

Diploma Programmes

The department offers three year diploma course approved by AICTE is Affiliated to Board of Technical Education, UP. The course consists of 6 semesters (proposed). The progress of the students is monitored semester wise through two Sessional Exams (Internal) and one End Semester Exam conducted by the Board of Technical Education. All the exams consist of testing theoretical knowledge and practical implementation.

The students are exposed to Mechanical Drafting, Designing, Modification of existing designs, Estimating and Costing. Operating various machines and their maintenance. They also get adequate exposure to latest technology such as, CAD/CAM, Robotics and CNC.

This course aims at producing highly qualified, efficient technicians capable of meeting the requirements of the basic level. During the course of study, the student is exposed to various subjects of Engineering such as Planning, Drafting (both manual and computer-aided), Surveying. Estimation & Costing. Construction Technology, Basics of Highway, Railway, Bridge, Airport, Tunnel and Harbour Engineering. The course also offers adequate exposure to Reinforced. Concrete Structures, Quality Control, Material Testing. Construction Management & Entrepreneurship. Professional Practice and Office Procedures. Students also get a feel of Extensive Survey, Building Drawing, Irrigation & Bridge Drawing and Structural Engineering Drawing. Students are also exposed to software like AUTOCAD & STAAD.

Diploma in Electrical Engineering is a 3 year skill- enabling engineering programme that students can pursue after completing class 10. The course deals with installation, maintenance, troubleshooting, magnetism, control systems, signal processing and telecommunications.
